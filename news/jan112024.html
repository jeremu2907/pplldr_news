Navigating the Corporate Landscape:

Overcoming Challenges in Military-to-Civilian Transition

The Problem

Military Veterans experience two significant challenges when they decide to transition from military service to the civilian corporate world. The first challenge is translating their military experiences, position duties, and credentials into the civilian corporate equivalents. Many books are available on this effort required, and there are many organizations that assist transitioning Veterans.

However, the second most significant challenge faced is that of documenting and then communicating their quality people leading skills and experiences. Although leadership assessments or 360-assessments are not new to Veterans, PplLdr.com provides the portability of being able to document and show the type of quality people leader you are based upon your prior and current direct reports' / team members' assessment of your leadership across more than 20 attributes.

How PplLdr Can Showcase Your Leadership

Using PplLdr ensures that Veterans can provide potential corporate recruiters with objective proof that you will add value to their organizations as a quality people leader based upon your prior military service. That's because the military is a leadership development queue, where we are taught, coached, and mentored to accomplish the mission while also ensuring we take care of our people that we lead.

This can be showcased to the public and provide a competitive edge to Veterans since you can link your PplLdr assessments and profile to other social and professional media - recruiters and corporate organizations now do not need to guess who the better candidate is for an offer because they can see this assessment as part of their decision process.

Conclusion

Use PplLdr to document your leadership and communicate your value as a quality people leader to recruiters, potential future direct reports / team members, and the public.
