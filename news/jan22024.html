How PplLdr Can Further Your Career

We strongly believe that quality people leaders are developed. A quality people leader is one that provides a vision and target state for all of the teams to follow, builds relationships with their direct reports / team members, and aligns all through teaching, coaching, and mentoring their team in achieving that target state.

Quality people leaders successfully influence their team toward achieving goals where they feel engaged and part of their organization's success: top talent of a quality people leader feel included, valued, supported, and empowered to succeed - they're not focused exclusively on financial metrics as a measurement of success.

Bad managers account for nearly 70% of variance in employee engagement scores according to studies conducted by Gallup. Unfortunately, Gallup also found that organizations fail to select the candidate with the right talent for leadership roles roughly 82% of the time! Bad managers cost businesses billions of dollars that cannot be recovered and reinforce the maxim of people quitting their bosses, not their jobs.

PplLdr not only showcases who the quality people leaders are that have the right talent to lead an organization's people, but we provide content to further your growth and development.
